Our Story

AYUSUTRA began with one simple realization: most wellness platforms provide generic advice, while Ayurveda requires precision by constitution. We built a care model where every patient is assessed as an individual, not a template.

Our platform creates a direct bridge between patients and certified Vaidyas, with structured diagnosis, clear treatment plans, and measurable follow-up.
